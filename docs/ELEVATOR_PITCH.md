# Elevator Pitch

## One-Line Pitch

CipherLink prevents supply chain finance fraud by detecting hidden invoice network risks in real time before money is disbursed.

## 10–15 Second Version

CipherLink is a pre-disbursement fraud intelligence platform for SCF. It combines invoice fingerprinting, graph anomaly detection, and explainable risk scoring to stop duplicates, circular trades, and high-risk financing events before losses happen.
